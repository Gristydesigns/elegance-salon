ABOUT

Elegance was established by owner Ciaranne Forde in early 2011, in the heart of the beautiful market town of Ledbury Herefordshire. With our team of highly qualified hair stylists and our excellence in customer service, we at Elegance make sure our clients receive the professional experience they deserve, whilst being able to relax in a friendly atmosphere.
