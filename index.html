ABOUT

Elegance was opened by owner Ciaranne Forde in early 2011, in the heart of

qualified hair stylists and our excellence in customer service, we at Elegance

make sure our clients receive the professional experience they deserve, whilst

being able to relax in a friendly atmosphere.
